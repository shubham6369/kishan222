# Cashfree Compliance Resolution Guide

Your Cashfree account was likely flagged due to terminology that resembled "Multi-Level Marketing (MLM)" or "High-Risk Investment" schemes (e.g., "Referral Income", "Membership Fee").

We have updated the platform to use compliant, society-based language. Below is the draft you should send to Cashfree support to resolve the "Category Mismatch" error.

---

## Support Ticket Draft

**Subject:** Response to Compliance Audit: Resolution of Category Mismatch for [Your Merchant Name/ID]

**Message:**

Dear Cashfree Compliance Team,

I am writing to provide clarification regarding our business model and the nature of transactions on our platform, **Kishan Seva Samiti**, following the recent "Category Mismatch" flag.

We have audited our platform's messaging and made comprehensive updates to ensure absolute transparency and compliance with your merchant guidelines.

### 1. Business Nature: Registered Farmer Society
Kishan Seva Samiti is a registered non-profit society dedicated to farmer empowerment and collective bargaining. We are NOT an investment platform or an MLM scheme. We operate as a marketplace and service provider for the agricultural community.

### 2. Nature of the ₹50 Payment
The ₹50 transaction collected during registration is a one-time **"Smart ID Card Issuance & Logistics Fee"**. 
- This fee covers the cost of generating a secure, digital-first farmer identity card.
- It covers the administrative and logistics costs of maintaining the farmer database.
- It is a service fee, not a "membership investment."

### 3. Community Rewards Program
We have renamed our "Referral" system to **"Community Rewards"**.
- This is a social outreach initiative to encourage farmers to bring their peers into the collective.
- Rewards are small incentives for community building, standard in many legitimate SaaS and service platforms.
- There is no "chain-link" or "multi-level" payout structure.

### 4. Website Updates
We have updated our website ([Your Website URL]) to reflect these professional standards. You will find that all references to "Income" or "Fees" have been replaced with "Rewards" and "Processing Fees" respectively.

We request you to re-review our platform under the **"NGO / Social Service / Marketplace"** category. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of financial compliance.

Thank you for your support in helping us serve the farming community.

Best regards,

[Your Name]
[Your Merchant ID]
